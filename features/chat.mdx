The Chat feature in Product Graph provides an intelligent conversational interface for working with your product documentation. Ask questions, refine requirements, and explore ideas with an AI assistant that understands your product context.

- Project context and goals

- Historical decisions and their rationale

## Using Chat

<Steps>
<Step title="Start a conversation">
  Open the chat panel from any document or project. Your current context is automatically included.
</Step>

<Step title="Ask questions or share ideas">
  Type your question or idea naturally. The AI understands product management
  terminology and can help with: - Clarifying requirements - Suggesting
  improvements - Identifying edge cases - Generating documentation
</Step>

<Step title="Apply suggestions">
  When the AI suggests changes or generates content, you can apply it directly to your documents with one click.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Example Prompts

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Requirements refinement">
