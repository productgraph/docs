## Review Workflows

<Steps>
<Step title="Share for review">
  Share documents with specific team members or groups. Set permissions for view-only or edit access.
</Step>

<Step title="Collect feedback">
  Reviewers add comments, suggest changes, and flag issues directly in the
  document.
</Step>

<Step title="Iterate and resolve">
  Address feedback, make updates, and resolve comments. The conversation history
  is preserved for reference.
</Step>

<Step title="Finalize">
  When everyone agrees, mark the document as approved and ready for development.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Team Organization

<Tabs>
<Tab title="Workspaces">
  Organize your team into workspaces with shared projects, documents, and settings. Each workspace has its own:
  - Member management
  - Permission settings
  - Templates and standards
</Tab>

<Tab title="Roles">
  Assign roles to control access: - **Admin**: Full access to workspace settings
  and all content - **Editor**: Can create and edit documents - **Viewer**:
  Read-only access to shared content
</Tab>

<Tab title="Sharing">
  Control document visibility:
  - **Private**: Only you can access
  - **Team**: All workspace members can access
  - **Specific people**: Share with selected individuals
</Tab>
</Tabs>

## Notifications

Stay informed about activity that matters to you:

- **Mentions**: When someone tags you in a comment
- **Comments**: On documents you're watching
- **Edits**: To documents you own or follow
- **Approvals**: When documents are approved or need your review

<Tip>
  Customize your notification preferences to avoid noise while staying informed
  about critical updates.
</Tip>
