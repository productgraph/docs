The Product Graph Editor is purpose-built for creating product documentation. It combines the simplicity of modern writing tools with powerful features for structuring requirements and specifications.

## Writing Experience

The editor provides a clean, distraction-free writing environment with:

- **Rich text formatting** for clear, readable documents
- **Markdown support** for those who prefer keyboard-driven writing
- **Slash commands** for quick access to formatting and components
- **AI assistance** integrated directly into your writing flow

## AI-Powered Writing

The editor includes AI capabilities that help you write better documentation:

<Steps>
<Step title="Inline suggestions">
  Get real-time suggestions as you write. The AI can complete sentences, suggest improvements, or expand on your ideas.
</Step>

<Step title="Content generation">
  Highlight a section and ask the AI to expand, summarize, or rephrase it.
  Generate acceptance criteria, edge cases, or technical details.
</Step>

<Step title="Quality checks">
  Run AI-powered checks to identify ambiguous language, missing requirements, or inconsistencies with other documents.
</Step>
</Steps>
